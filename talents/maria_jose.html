María José Quintanilla

Biography

María José Quintanilla Sandoval was born on February 17 in Maipú, Santiago, Chile. She is the youngest of three brothers. She grew up with her family in the commune of Maipú, since she was a child she was fond of singing. Since she was very small, she was a great admirer of Mexican music, her father was responsible for instilling that culture.

February 17 of the year 2001 participated in the International Festival of Mexican music Puyehue, staying with the first place in Children's Category. In July of the same year, the Brave International Brave Children's International Talent contest was presented, being selected to travel to Italy, representing our country in September with the Zingara theme.

At the end of the same month is presented in a casting, being selected to be part of the cast of the new TVN program Red Fama Contrafama (First Generation). Where she stood out for his talent and short age. She obtained the fourth place of the competition, and the distinction to the most popular, the prize would be to record his long-awaited disc of ranchera music. In the month of March, when she was only 13 years old, the recordings of his first album began and it was like that after working hard for a whole month, the great day arrived; On April 1, 2003, in the Red Program, his first album is released, which bears the name of MEXICO CUTE AND DEAR. The one that was record of sale in our country, in addition was awarded with the prize ARTIST REVELATION 2003 SONY MUSIC SUR.

On February 21, 2004, one of the most important stages of the country is climbed for the first time, it finally arrives at the longed-for festival of the vineyard of the sea, that was the great dream of María José, that night we dazzled her with all her talent , appearing before more than 15 thousand people who were in the fifth Vergara, and became worthy of all the prizes that were given in that contest. silver and gold torch, silver and gold seagull. With them his great dream was fulfilled despite his short 14 years of age, September was the month of 2004, and María José travels to Brazil where she takes theater classes which later would serve to participate in the great work musical the Wizard of oz. Where I play the unforgettable dorothy, was on stage for almost 3 months being a success.

In 2005, she released his love transition album, in which she left the world of rancheras a little behind. Later in the same year she debuted for the first time as a television host and nothing more nor less than with a program called ROJITO having very good acceptance by the audience, finished that process, with animated the program runs video.

The year 2006 began to record the film RED THE MOVIE, the one that premiered on July 13 of the same year, in it she reveals her other acting skills, in this film I play the dreamy Carmencita.

In the summer of 2007 the second season of the Rojito program airs where she stands out as the host of this children's program.

As of March 2007, she began to animate a children's program called TRONIA that, thanks to the good results obtained, extended throughout 2007.

In July 2007 his fourth album is released, which took about a year and a half to produce, in this project was guided by Daniel Guerrero, the record consists of eleven songs in which two were composed by her, we also see her in a completely different facet since in this work she dares to compose and take your guitar to interpret the themes that compose it. His first single "Tu Corazón" was number one in the radio rankings. It is with this material that is released in Mexico, the first days of January 2008. Starting a series of activities in Guadalajara, Zacatecas and Aguas Calientes.

In March 2008, she first visited Tapatías lands, where for two weeks she visited different media outlets in Guadalajara and Zacatecas.

In June of the same year, she travels back to Mexico, to record what will be his fifth production, which will be called "Hoja en Blanco", an album that is produced entirely in Guadalajara, and that spans three genres; Mariachi, Cumbia Ranchera and Banda, whose single is among the most important rankings in Chile and which in Mexico sounds in the states of Aguas "ARREPENTIDA" is the new single by María José Quintanilla, from her new album "BANDOLERA" which will be published in what remains of this year 2010, "ARREPENTIDA" is a song dedicated to all those women who are tired of the mistreatment suffered by their spouses they decide to take justice into their own hands, product of which they are deprived of freedom, the video of this song was recorded in the jail "SANTIAGO UNO".
