Banda Cruz De Oro

Biography

Banda Cruz de Oro or BCDO is originally from Sinaloa, Mexico, its members are from different areas of the state, so their sounds mix different environments that make them completely authentic.

BCDO was born in 2009 as an initiative of Jorge Vargas who, captivated by the musical world, wanted to form his own band and gather musicians of the region to carry out his dream despite not being a musician. The name Cruz de Oro is assigned to the band as a reference to a good religious omen.
