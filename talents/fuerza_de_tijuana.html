Fuerza de Tijuana

Biography

Fuerza de Tijuana is a northern Mexican regional group that emerged in 2009 in Tijuana, Baja California. Héctor Elías (Accordion and first Voice) is the band’s leader who by then had great experience in the musical world thanks to the influence of his family and friends; who were musicians.

After dedicating himself to music for a long period of time and having tried with different friends, Héctor finally found his current bandmates in his neighborhood. Paúl Moreno (Drummer), Adrián Suarez (Bass and Second Voice) and Iván Ramos (Bajo Quinto) join as Fuerza de Tijuana and embark on the journey of their dreams.

8 years ago these young Americans with Mexican roots came together in the city of Tijuana and formed the musical group Fuerza de Tijuana that originally sang mainly rock in Spanish and regional Mexican, however for the new millennium movement of the altered corridos infected So much the public of Baja California that they were no exception. By then they began to sing in the nightclubs of the border city, singing some covers of Gerardo Ortiz and Larry Hernández, among others. In 2011 they metr Tony Larios and after a brief consideration he decided to manage their talent in a professional manner.

Motivated by its audience Fuerza de Tijuana begins to make its own compositions and after its first albums New Pistoleros in 2011, Mi Frontera (2013), Escoge Deluxe Edition (2014), comes the single "El Americano" that launches them to the fame with millions of reproductions in social networks and great popularity in the radio of the United States and Mexico.

The run "El Americano" opened the doors the public of both countries, making them deserving of two awards in 2015 Street Awards as Corrido del Año and Grupo con Mejor Futuro. Among their successes, Fuerza de Tijuana was chosen as the best profiled northern group to attend the music and rhythm conference at the University of San Diego to talk about their music. There, the members highlighted the importance of being the voice of society through art and how its rhymes transmit social awareness.

Then in 2016 they released their album La Nueva Cara from Tijuana, with which their run "El Benny" debuted in the first popularity lists, giving continuity to the success that Fuerza de Tijuana already brought. Likewise, their single "El Popeye" ranked # 6 on Latino Monitor Chart and # 9 on BDS.

Their second most recent single "Caro lo Pagué" was in the Top 10 of Monitor Latino and their reality show Así es El Show already counts more than half a million views on YouTube with six episodes since its premiere in August.

Fuerza de Tijuana has shared the stage with Maná, Kinky, Prince Royce, Untouchable, Los Inquietos del Norte, Espinoza Paz, Larry Hernández, among others. They are admirers of Los Cadetes de Linares, Juan Villareal, Los Cachorros and Maná. As a group they have toured in the main cities of the United States and Mexico.
