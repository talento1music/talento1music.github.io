Alberto Stylee

Biography

Carlos Alberto Pizarro, singer-author known artistically as "Alberto Stylee" was born in Río Piedras, Puerto Rico. His artistic career begins 1995 within the Rap and Reggae genres. Alberto Stylee has recorded for DJs such as Playero, Nico Canada, DJ Eric, DJ Nelson, DJ Blass, Looney Toons, DJ Barbosa and others. His peculiar style and voice launched him to immediate success, opening the doors to productions such as Playero 39, Playero 40, Playero 41 and Playero 42, Boricua Guerrero, White Lion, Las Gargolas and La Conspiración that have sold thousands of copies. He gained international recognition with his famous collab "DJ Nelson and Alberto Stylee" reaching great popularity within Puerto Rico and Latin America.

In 1999 he released his first solo production titled "Exclusive”. This production was nominated in the category for best "Rap and Reggae Album" of the year in the Tu Música awards. In 2003 he left Puerto Rico in search of new horizons and settled in the city of Miami, Florida. His most recent single is “Vamonos Ya”.
