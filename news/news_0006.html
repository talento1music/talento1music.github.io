Anuel AA Confirmed for a Q&A at Billboard's Latin Music Week, Registration Is Now Open

JANUARY 14, 2019




Registration is now open for Billboard’s 29th annual Latin Music Week which, this year, returns to The Venetian in Las Vegas on April 22-25. Latin Music Week, the most respected conference and award show in Latin music, will bring the top artists, executives and power players in the industry together for a week of concerts, showcases, interviews, keynotes and industry panels.

Puerto Rican trap star Anuel AA has been confirmed to sit down for an exclusive one-on-one interview.

“We are proud to always be ahead of the curve, says Leila Cobo, Billboard’s Executive Director of Content and Programming for Latin Music and Entertainment. “We were the first media outlet to interview Anuel AA the day he walked out a free man, and now, we will be the media outlet to celebrate nine months of extraordinary achievements.”

Latin Music Week features Q&A sessions and panels, including its signature “Star Q&As” as well as panels focusing on streaming, videos, mainstream and Latin collaborations and more. The lineup will also include the highly-anticipated return of the Women’s Panel as well as “Machismo and Feminism,” a look at contemporary regional Mexican music.

Registration to the conference will allow attendees access to three days of panels, one-on-one sessions and showcases, plus tickets to the Billboard Latin Music Awards, which will air live on Telemundo on April 25.

To register for the conference and awards, visit BillboardLatinMusicWeek.com. Special loyalty and early bird rates are available.
